Genre: Pop / Synth‑Pop

1989

Release Year: 2014
Label: Big Machine Records
Producers: Max Martin, Shellback, Ryan Tedder, Jack Antonoff

1989 marked Taylor Swift’s full transition from country to pop, drawing influence from 1980s synth‑pop. The album emphasised clean, radio‑friendly production and themes of independence, fame, and relationships, becoming one of the decade’s most commercially successful records.
